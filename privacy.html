Pass Checker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Pass Checker is an independent road condition monitoring app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you. This policy explains what minimal data is involved in using the app.

Data we do not collect

Pass Checker does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

There are no user accounts, no sign-ups, and no login.

Data stored on your device

The app saves the following preferences locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Theme preference (dark or light mode)
  • Text size and bold text preferences
  • Disclaimer acknowledgment status

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser or app storage in your device settings.

Network requests

When you use the app, it makes the following outbound requests:

  • To our backend API to fetch road condition data from state DOT agencies — no personal data is sent
  • To state DOT image servers to load traffic camera images — no personal data is sent

No IP address logging or user tracking occurs on our backend.

External links

The app contains links to third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Buy Me a Coffee (buymeacoffee.com) — a voluntary tip service
  • State DOT websites — the agencies that provide road condition data

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

Children's privacy

Pass Checker does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will reflect the revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at hello@mountainmediadigital.com
